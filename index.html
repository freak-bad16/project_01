Meet Dr. Puja Shukla

(MPT, MD (A.M.), DNHE, MCHN, DNYS, DAP, Garbhsanskar) – Expert in Pain, Weight & Women’s Wellness Like _ PCOD, PCOS infertility & period pain Specialist in Kanpur

Dr. Puja Shukla is a trusted Women’s Health Expert, Nutritionist, and Lifestyle Disorder Specialist in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. She is known for her expertise in natural healing, preventive healthcare, and holistic well-being, offering non-invasive, result-oriented therapies that target the root cause of health issues.

With years of clinical experience, she provides advanced Pain Management solutions for back pain, neck pain, knee pain, and sciatica without surgery. Her Weight Reduction & Nutrition Programs are customized to help clients lose weight, improve metabolism, and boost energy naturally.

She is also an expert in Pregnancy & Postnatal Care, including pelvic floor rehabilitation, hormonal balance, and post-delivery recovery. As a Lifestyle Disorder Reversal Specialist, she works on conditions like diabetes, thyroid disorders, hypertension, migraine, and PCOD/PCOS through personalized diet, therapy, and lifestyle modification.
