About Us

Welcome To Kapsveda Wellness Clinic

Welcome to Kapsveda Wellness Clinic, your trusted destination for holistic healing and wellness. Located in Kalyanpur, Kanpur, our clinic is dedicated to providing integrative healthcare solutions that combine modern medical science with traditional healing practices. We specialize in pain management, physiotherapy, weight management, and lifestyle therapy, helping individuals achieve their optimal health and well-being. At Kapsveda Wellness Clinic, we believe in a patient-centric approach that focuses on personalized treatment plans tailored to individual needs. Whether you are dealing with chronic pain, recovering from an injury, seeking weight loss solutions, or looking for pregnancy and postnatal fitness programs, we offer comprehensive and customized care to ensure the best outcomes.

Our Founder

Meet Dr. Puja Shukla

MPT, MD(AM), DNHE, MIMCHN, DNYS, DAP

Dr. Puja Shukla is the founder and leading specialist at Kapsveda Wellness Clinic. With extensive experience in pain management, physiotherapy, and holistic wellness, she has been transforming lives since 2015. She holds multiple prestigious qualifications, including MPT, MD (AM), DNHE, MIMCHN, DNYS, and DAP, which reflect her expertise in various health disciplines.

Dr. Shukla is a renowned pain and weight management expert known for her integrative approach to healthcare. She is a proud member of the Association of Manual Orthopedic Physical Therapists (AOMPT) and has contributed significantly to research and publications in the field of health and wellness. Her deep understanding of both modern and alternative medicine allows her to offer innovative treatments that provide lasting relief and long-term health benefits.
