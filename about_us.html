Meet Dr. Puja Shukla

Dr. Puja Shukla is the founder and leading specialist at Kapsveda Wellness Clinic. With extensive experience in pain management, physiotherapy, and holistic wellness, she has been transforming lives since 2015. She holds multiple prestigious qualifications, including MPT, MD (AM), DNHE, MIMCHN, DNYS, and DAP, which reflect her expertise in various health disciplines.

Dr. Shukla is a renowned pain and weight management expert known for her integrative approach to healthcare. She is a proud member of the Association of Manual Orthopedic Physical Therapists (AOMPT) and has contributed significantly to research and publications in the field of health and wellness. Her deep understanding of both modern and alternative medicine allows her to offer innovative treatments that provide lasting relief and long-term health benefits.
